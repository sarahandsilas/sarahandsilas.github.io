Who are Sarah and Silas?

Festival sweethearts Sarah and Silas play folk roots music.

Their love, skill and connection shine through exciting dueling fiddles and sweet vocal harmonies which dance to the strum of a ukulele and the beat of a drum. Their catchy original songs and tunes will move your feet and warm your heart.

As seen in The Royal High Jinx and The Bushwackers, don't miss them at a festival near you!
