Who are Sarah & Silas?

Festival sweethearts Sarah & Silas play folk roots music.

Their love, skill and connection shine through exciting dualling fiddles and sweet vocal harmonies which dance to the strum of a ukulele and the beat of a drum. Their catchy original songs and tunes will move your feet and warm your heart.

As seen in The Royal High Jinx and The Bushwackers, don't miss them at a festival near you!
